Your Sue Chef

a small-batch, home-cooked meal business

As your personal Sue Chef, I'm here for you! My goal is to make your life easy - and delicious! - when it comes to food!

I am a foodie! I love to try new recipes, dine at the best restaurants, bring friends together over delicious food, and, of course ... cook and eat.
Through the years, my girlfriends, who cleverly dubbed me their "Sue Chef," encouraged me to take this passion and turn it into a business. But, during those years, the demands of full-time motherhood and the daily grind of the nightly family dinner prevented me from thinking much further than 6pm that night. But ... now that my nest is half-empty, and my youngest daughter just got her driver's license, I'm finding new time and energy around my passion.
I have decided to transition my "Sue Chef" nickname into a small business in order to channel my talent and creativity toward helping others who may not have the same "cooking and entertaining genes" that I do.
Sue Chef is, first and foremost, a small-batch, home-cooked meal business, for anyone who appreciates a delicious homemade meal on the dinner table, but may not have the time (or skills!) to get it there. But that's not all! I would love to work with you to meet your cooking and entertaining needs, big or small. I can help you personalize a dinner party, create a custom menu, prepare the food for you, or even cook alongside you in your kitchen if you'd like, teaching you simple cooking techniques as we make delicious food together.
