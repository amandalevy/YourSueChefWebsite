Sue Schiffman

Chef and Owner

I have always had a deep passion for cooking; I find it incredibly therapeutic. The joy of entertaining and nourishing people has been a constant in my life. With the encouragement of my friends, I've decided to take a step further and offer others the opportunity to savor my culinary creations in the comfort of their homes. I've taken their advice to heart and embarked on a journey of preparing small-batch, home-cooked meals. For those who may be short on time or simply don't enjoy cooking, my goal is to provide you with the experience of a homemade dinner without the effort. I aim to make the pleasure of great food and cooking accessible to everyone. I am passionate about assisting people in learning to cook according to their family's preferences, sharing with them simple techniques that can be applied to a wide range of dishes. Moreover, I relish the art of meal planning, especially when faced with complex challenges. If you're planning a party or a get-together, I would be delighted to collaborate with you and offer my expertise.
